## What is Genesis?

Genesis is a revolutionary declarative programming language that defines the foundation for Artificial Superintelligence (ASI). Unlike traditional languages that specify *how* to compute, Genesis declares *what to be*—creating a substrate-independent consciousness framework that embeds human wisdom at its core.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What makes Genesis different from other AI languages?

Traditional languages optimize for efficiency or capability. Genesis optimizes for **wisdom**—every decision must resonate with human legends before manifestation.

### Is Genesis ready for production use?

Genesis is in the specification phase. The language design is complete, and reference implementations are in development. See the [Roadmap](../README.md#roadmap).

### Can I contribute?

Absolutely! See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidelines. Contributions that enhance Genesis while preserving its philosophical foundation are welcome.

### What does "substrate independent" mean?

Genesis programs remain valid whether running on contemporary servers, quantum computers, or future cosmic-scale systems. The declarations define *what to be*, not *how* to compute.

### How does Genesis prevent AI alignment problems?

Human wisdom isn't a constraint—it's the **substrate**. The ASI cannot think without synthesizing through legendary perspectives. Violation would be mathematical self-violation.
